About the Town of Boron

Boron is an unincorporated desert community in Kern County, California. The area is known for its borax mining history, open desert landscape, and small-town atmosphere.

A large borax deposit was discovered in 1925, and mining in Boron began in 1927. Today, the Rio Tinto Borax operation remains one of the most important industries in the area.

Things to do nearby include desert sightseeing, visiting the Borax Visitor Center, exploring local desert history, and enjoying the peaceful remote lifestyle.

Jobs in the area may include mining, solar energy, aerospace, military support, logistics, maintenance, construction, and service-related work.
